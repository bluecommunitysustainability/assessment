  • Our Mission

    We aim to challenge the negative impacts of tourism by providing a place where destination communities can collaboratively measure, report, track and analyze local sustainability efforts, as a team. Each team is assigned a color which represents the destination members of that team have permissions to edit, update or contribute to.

    Our holistic sustainability assessment focuses on a strategic matrix of inquiries regarding local sustainability efforts, practices and policies. Teams decide for themselves how to best delegate the sectional responsibilities. Each section is represented by a single emoji, containing metric questions related to the represented theme.
