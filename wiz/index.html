  • Track Your Sustainability Efforts & Progress From Now until Forever: Our Mission

    A System of Accountability and Transparency


    Establish a baseline for your community by completing a series of questions, based on themes in sustainability. The Blue Community Assessment app allows the work of data collection, entry and update to be distributed across a team. All destinations are assigned a team color. When assessors log in, the system already knows that team they belong to and which criteria they are permitted to edit/update/access. Once all 133 questions have been given an initial answer, 100% of baseline will be complete. From that moment forward, everything is tracked based on updates provided to the assessment over time, activating Historical Data modules and such.

    No more greenwashing. All edits made to the destination data are time stamped and the change becomes attributed to the person making the change. This data is only viewable by the members belonging to the same team. This change becomes archived to our database and recorded as a new change within the blockchain. The blue community blockchain keeps a record of every change which happens to the data over time, beginning from the date baseline was established. We aim to challenge the negative impacts of tourism by providing a place where destination communities can collaboratively measure, report, track and analyze local sustainability efforts, as a team. Each team is assigned a color which represents the destination members of that team have permissions to edit, update or contribute to.

    Our holistic sustainability assessment focuses on a strategic matrix of inquiries regarding local sustainability efforts, practices and policies. Teams decide for themselves how to best delegate the sectional responsibilities. Each section is represented by a single emoji, containing metric questions related to the represented theme.

  • Give the Stakeholders a Reason to Trust You

    Help the community trust you by providing the transparency stakeholders seek. Along with providing reference links to claims made inside the assessment, you can upload photos, videos, documents and evidence. When your destination provides demonstrable proof of improvement and implementations, the public will have a way to see you accomplish your established goals in real time. Blue Community will help your team with your plan and everything you need to succeed with your team efforts. Each section has a place for recording "Notes to Stakeholders" This is where goals can be announced and visible to stakeholders of your community. Whenever a goal set by a team is achieved, it is eligible to become a best practice for other communities to try locally.

    Add a Destination to Blue Community

    Best practices are the backbone for action in Blue Communities. They help us stand up and look all the way around at where we are, where we need to be and how we get there. It is very simple to record a number and providing proof isn't difficult either. It is the act of setting a goal to improve which moves the needle. It is knowing the answer to a question so well that you cannot wait for the new data to become available.

    Everything is connected. Assessment questions provide direct integrated reference to the related GSTC criteria as well as the UNWTO Sustainable Development Goals and the 12 Blue Community strategies co-developed with Walt Disney Environmental & Wildlife divisions.. The SDGs also provide relationship to both questions and GSTC criteria.

  • Learn from Best Practices in other destinations within the system.

    Track your destination’s progress over time, as a system, and engage your stakeholders in a in a transparent and easy to manage assessment application. When something improves within a metric group, your Destination will have the opportunity to add a Best Practice to the Blue Community Assessment system.
